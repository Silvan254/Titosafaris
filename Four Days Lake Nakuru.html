Itenary:
Day 1 Nairobi to Maasai Mara

  • o 7 – 8 a.m. pick up from your overnight accommodation or central place in the CBD
  • Departure for an enjoyable journey south along the peak and floor of the Great Rift Valley. We will stop at critical viewpoints for a good view, to take some breath, photo opportunity and optional souvenirs/post cards.
  • Early lunch at Narok town, the Maasai Capital, and for some early glimpse into the Maasai people.
  • Arrival in the Maasai Mara for late afternoon game drive till late evening to watch unique sunset over the Mara.
  • Dinner and overnight stay at your tented camp. Optional drinks in the camp

Itenary:
Day 2 Full Day Maasai Mara Safari to Wildebeest Migration Path

  • Early breakfast at the camp then 8 AM departure for full day safari exploring Africa’s flora and fauna, and searching for the Big Five
  • Drive to the Mara River on Kenya/Tanzania boarder for the wildebeest migration. The opposite of the river is Serengeti in Tanzania.
  • Picnic lunch will be served by the river as you enjoy a view of hippos, crocodiles and wildebeests.
  • Late evening drive to catch a view of all the Big Five
  • Dinner and overnight stay at your tented camp

Itenary:
Day 3 Maasai Village, Maasai Mara to Lake Nakuru National Park.

  • Pre-breakfast game drive between 6.30 a.m – 8.30 a.m.
  • Breakfast at the camp then departure to Maasai village for sampling of authentic Maasai culture
  • Departure for Lake Nakuru also known as the PINK LAKE for its great masses of pink flamingo .
  • Enjoy picturesque view of the lake which is home to endangered WHITE and BLACK RHINOS and over 350 bird species. It is an Ornithological Paradise!
  • Overnight stay in Nakuru town.

Day 4 Lake Nakuru National Park to Nairobi

  • Early morning full course breakfast
  • Tour of Lake Nakuru Rhino sanctuary for morning game drive
  • Departure for Nairobi with optional airport transfer for those catching early evening flights
